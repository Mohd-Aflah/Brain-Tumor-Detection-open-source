Key Capabilities

Designed for precision, speed, and educational accessibility.

⚡

Fast AI Inference

Utilizes optimized YOLO models for near real-time segmentation of tumor regions directly from MRI image files.

📊

Detailed Analytics

Calculates crucial metrics including tumor area percentage, estimated volume, and multi-tumor detection.

🔒

Local & Secure

Runs entirely on your local machine. No images are uploaded to external cloud services, ensuring absolute privacy.

🖨️

Medical Reports

Generates printable, formatted summary reports of the AI analysis for academic and research documentation.

About The Project

This project was developed to bridge the gap between complex deep learning models and accessible user interfaces for medical image analysis.

It provides a complete pipeline from image upload to YOLO-based semantic segmentation, concluding with analytical assessment. It is perfectly suited for college curriculum, bioinformatics research, and AI enthusiasts.

  • Python & Flask
  • Ultralytics YOLO (PyTorch)
  • OpenCV & NumPy
  • HTML5, CSS3, JS
